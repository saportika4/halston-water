SECTORS WE SERVE

Reliable Water Solutions for Every Industry

From desalination to wastewater recycling, Halston delivers tailored, cost-effective water treatment systems across key sectors in the UAE and beyond.

Municipal & Government

Drinking water plants, wastewater treatment, and large-scale desalination for cities and public utilities.

Hospitality & Tourism

Hotels, resorts, and villas – grey water recycling, swimming pool treatment and high-quality drinking water systems.

Industrial & Manufacturing

Process water, boiler feed water, and wastewater treatment for factories and heavy industries.

Oil & Gas

Produced water treatment, desalination and high-purity water solutions for offshore and onshore facilities.

Construction & Labour Camps

Containerized RO plants and mobile water treatment units for remote camps and construction sites.

Pharmaceutical & Healthcare

Ultrapure water and deionization systems for hospitals, laboratories and pharmaceutical production.

Food & Beverage

High-quality process water and wastewater treatment for food processing and beverage industries.

Textile & Dyeing

Effluent treatment and water recycling systems for textile and dyeing industries.

Halston Water Equip. Ind. Co. LLC provides complete water and wastewater treatment solutions — from design and manufacturing to installation and long-term maintenance — for clients across the UAE and internationally.
