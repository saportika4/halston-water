OUR PRODUCTS

Premium Water & Wastewater Treatment Systems

HALSTON TECHNOLOGIES has the capacity to provide low cost solutions for the following types of water and waste water treatment systems:

Tap Water Purification Systems

Advanced purification systems designed to deliver clean, safe drinking water for residential, commercial and industrial use.

Reverse Osmosis Desalination Systems

High-efficiency RO plants for seawater, brackish water and tap water desalination.

Deionization Systems

High-purity water systems for laboratories, pharmaceutical, electronics and industrial process applications.

Grey Water Treatment

Advanced recycling systems for wastewater from showers, sinks and laundry for reuse in irrigation and flushing.

Filtration Systems

Media filtration and Ultrafiltration systems that remove suspended solids, turbidity, colour and microorganisms.

Containerized Reverse Osmosis System

Plug-and-play, mobile containerized RO plants ideal for remote sites and emergency use.

Ultraviolet Sterilizer

UV disinfection systems that effectively eliminate bacteria, viruses and microorganisms without chemicals.

Specialty Chemicals

High-performance water treatment chemicals for scale control, corrosion inhibition, biocides and membrane cleaning.
