ABOUT HALSTON WATER

Your Trusted Water Treatment Partner

Halston Water Equip. Ind. Co. LLC (HW) is a leading provider of complete water and wastewater treatment solutions in the UAE and internationally. With over 15 years of expertise, we specialize in the design, manufacturing, installation, and commissioning of advanced RO desalination, filtration, grey water recycling, and containerized treatment systems.

Our systems are built with premium-grade components to deliver maximum reliability, regulatory compliance, and superior water quality for commercial, industrial, municipal, and agricultural applications.
