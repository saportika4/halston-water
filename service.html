WHAT WE DO

Our Core Services

Halston Water specialises in designing, manufacturing, and commissioning water purification systems for commercial, industrial and municipal sectors.

Service 01

Design & Engineering

Custom design and precision engineering of water and wastewater treatment systems for any scale.

Service 02

System Manufacturing

In-house manufacturing of RO systems, filtration units and containerized treatment plants using quality-grade components.

Service 03

Installation & Commissioning

Professional on-site installation and commissioning by certified engineers with full testing and handover support.

Service 04

Technical Auditing & Consultation

Expert technical audits and consultation services to optimise existing water systems and ensure regulatory compliance.

Service 05

Operation & Maintenance

Scheduled and emergency maintenance, system monitoring, and long-term O&M contracts for domestic and international clients.

OUR TECHNOLOGY

Innovative Water Treatment Solutions

Cutting-edge technologies engineered for maximum reliability, efficiency and sustainability.

Reverse Osmosis (RO) Systems

Tap water, brackish water, and seawater desalination for drinking, irrigation and industrial process water.

Grey Water Treatment

Advanced treatment of wastewater from washers, bathtubs and showers for reuse in irrigation, car wash and toilet flushing.

Multimedia Filtration & Ultrafiltration

Removes suspended solids, turbidity, colour, organic matter and microorganisms from water.

Containerized Treatment Plants

Mobile, self-contained units ideal for construction camps, remote sites and emergency deployments.

Deionization & Specialty Chemicals

High-purity process water solutions for industrial, pharmaceutical and laboratory use.

Halston Water Equip. Ind. Co. LLC provides complete water and wastewater treatment solutions — from design and manufacturing to installation and long-term maintenance — for clients across the UAE and internationally.
