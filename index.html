ABOUT HALSTON WATER

Your Trusted Water Treatment Partner

Halston Water Equip. Ind. Co. LLC (HW) offers complete solutions for water and wastewater treatment applications and supplies high-quality related components. We are specialists in the design, installation, and commissioning of a comprehensive range of water treatment systems — including RO desalination, grey water, booster systems, and reuse applications — for commercial, industrial, municipal, and agricultural clients.

Our systems are engineered with precision-grade components to deliver maximum reliability and produce the highest quality drinking and process water, safeguarding the health and productivity of our customers.

Delivering pure water solutions across the UAE and beyond.

Innovative Water Treatment Solutions

Multimedia Filtration & Ultrafiltration Removes suspended solids, turbidity, colour, organic matter and microorganisms from water.
